💡 Key Insights

🕐 Shift Time Is the #1 Problem

48% of all kickbacks (106 incidents) are due to drivers running out of hours. Better load sequencing and earlier dispatch cutoffs could directly eliminate this.

🔧 Equipment Failures Are Undercounted

46 incidents (21%) involve trailer brakes, no available trailers, coolant leaks, and tire failures — most logged as "generic" and previously invisible.

📅 May 19 Was a Crisis Day

32 kickbacks in a single day — nearly 4× the average. This spike warrants investigation: terminal outage, staffing shortage, or weather event.

🚛 3 Drivers Own 19% of All Kickbacks

⛽ FuelCity & GPM Lead Site Kickbacks

🌙 Late Night & Early Morning Are High-Risk

Hours 22:00–06:00 account for a disproportionate share of kickbacks, when drivers are near end-of-shift and less likely to accept additional loads.
